About Me

I am a software engineer with a strong passion for continuous learning. My journey in software development began during my junior year of college, where my eagerness to learn helped me secure several internships, working as a web developer intern at various startups. I also completed a residency with the New York City Tech Talent Pipeline, where I collaborated with others to build full-stack web applications. I graduated magna cum laude from CUNY Brooklyn College with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.

Since graduating, I have been expanding my knowledge of enterprise development through self-directed learning and hands-on experience with my project, GroupHQ. My focus is on supporting efficient development practices within parallel teams and navigating the unique challenges of enterprise-level environments. Recently, I was accepted into the FastTrack program at Cook Systems, where I am training alongside other talented individuals to further enhance my enterprise development skills.
